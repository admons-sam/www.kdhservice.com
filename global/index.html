KDH use a sharper, high definition HUD projector. The image will be reflected into the rider’s vision using beam splitting glass, making the Driving experience look a lot more like what you’d imagine out of a motorcycle HUD than the previous "Classic" model.

The network, called KDHcloud, will help businesses process and manage data generated from billions of helmet devices and with their applications around the world in real time, and all content will become a real asset for safe drive with useful data, but also for security control.

Using the gestural interface, or voice control or even connecting the car-detector infrared sensor, the supportive information will assist you anytime. Content display on the visor larger field of vision, will make driving more safe and predictable.

KDH make possible the safe-driving by predicting dangers before they occur based on an accumulation of sensor data indicating fatigue, stress, and tension, and applying that to a hazard map.
