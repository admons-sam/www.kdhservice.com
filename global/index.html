About us

KDH delivers to anyone who's interested in joining the business various solutions of online ebook service, portable devices, including eReaders, tablets, and electronic book-bags. Well aware potential customers usually lack IT expertise, KDH Design House dedicates itself to make the best use of joint­development partners' resources, experiences, advantages and expertise to flourish in the mobile consumer electronics business.

KDH's hybrid DNA of the engineering house and the service vendor guarantees the most ideal solution for product design optimized for the mobile devices. Turning your requirements of marketing purposes into the technical and manufacturing specifications. We design and manage your projects in hardware and software development from the beginning to mass production, from logistics to the after­sales technical support process.

Founded in 2009, KDH Design House has formed a strong strategic partnership with the top tier Electronic Manufacturing Service (EMS) enterprises in the world, and thus has absolute advantages in manufacturing and quality assurance for various products. We play the role of your exclusive design house, as a single liaison that manages projects and the resources allocation in different parties for you. For a better local sales support, KDH was authorized by KOOBE since 2013 as the sales representative to manage the business development sustainable in the European market.
